About the workshop

Query performance prediction (QPP), a.k.a. query difficulty prediction, is a key task in information retrieval (IR). The task of QPP is defined as estimating search effectiveness without human relevance judgments.

In this workshop, we aim to bring together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss new perspectives on QPP. QPP++ 2025 is the continuation of the QPP++ 2023 workshop held in ECIR 2023
